Lasagna Recipe

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat on skillet till browned and crumbly.
  2. Cook the noodle: Boil the noodle in lightly salted water until they are al dante.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs and remaining parsley.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Layer ingredient according to recipe starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella.
  5. Bake the Lasagna: Cover it with foil and bake it in oven for about forty-five minutes.
